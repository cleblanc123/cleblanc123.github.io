The Chick-Fil-A Story

Does the idea of Chick-Fil-A make your mouth salivate? When you go on car rides to a place known to have Chick-Fil-A, do you look forward to eating there? When you go to Chick-Fil-A, do you always expect fast service and kind servers? Then you probably can get behind the history of Chick-Fil-A. Chick-Fil-A was created when a man named Truett Cathy learned a way to make chicken just as fast as burgers in a pressure-fryer!

Chick-Fil-A is a place where you can obtain fast food that is actually fast, while not losing quality in service or product. Chick-Fil-A makes breakfast, lunch, and dinner items for whenever you are on the road, or simplying desiring poultry goodness. The Breakfast menu offers a combination of chicken as well as egg based items to make satisfactory breakfast, and our hash-browns will have you digging your hands into the bag for more! Our Lunch menu mainly consists of Chicken products of many varations, including our popular Spicy Chicken Deluxe Sandwich.

Come see us at chick-fil-a.com!

Our happiness has spread to several more locations in New Orleans and elsewhere with beautiful premises. So come, and enjoy a Chick-Fil-A meal!
